POS Stock Realtime

Show the quantity on hand of product in POS screen and update real-time quantity on hand when there is a changee in Backend (if POS online)

POS Stock realtime was originally developed by the D.jane in Odoo11, we have migrated and improved it in Odoo12

POS Stock Realtime Key features:

  • Checkbox to configure to show quantity on hand of product in Stock. This checkbox is checked by default
  • Realtime update quantity on hand of product when SHOP is online. When there is a change of quantity in Backend, the quantity of that product in POS frontend automatically updated if POS is online
  • Checkbox to configure only show stock of product within current POS stock (in multi-warehouse context)
  • Checkbox to hide stock outside of current POS stock (in multi-warehouse context)
  • Limit quantity order in POS for some special cases
